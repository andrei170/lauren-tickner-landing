FAQ

Common Questions

Who is Impact School actually for?

Coaches, consultants, course creators, and service providers with proof of concept (typically £5K a month or more) who want to scale to multi-six or seven figures. We're not a fit for total beginners. There are better starting points for that.

How long is the programme?

Six months of structured mentorship. Most accepted operators see meaningful changes inside the first 60 days and compounding results across the full container.

What's the time commitment?

Roughly 3-5 hours per week between live calls, async coaching, and execution. Not a heavy time load on top of running your business. The framework is designed to integrate, not interrupt.

Why do you turn down so many applications?

To keep the room sharp. Operators progress faster when the rest of the room is at or above their level. We turn down applications that aren't ready, that don't have proof of concept yet, or that aren't financially set up to invest properly.

What does the application process look like?

Submit a 4-minute application. If you fit the criteria, a senior coach on the team books a qualification call within 48 hours. The call is honest both ways. If it's a fit we move forward. If it isn't, we'll point you somewhere better suited.
